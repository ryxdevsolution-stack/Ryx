## The Temptation of AI Website Builders

In 2025, you can describe your business to an AI and receive a fully designed, deployed website within minutes. Framer AI, Wix ADI, Squarespace, and Durable all offer this. They're genuinely impressive.

But impressive and effective are different things.

## What AI Website Builders Are Actually Good At

Be honest: for some businesses, an AI builder is the right answer.

**Use an AI builder if:**
- You need something live this week and have zero budget
- You're testing a new business idea and don't know if it'll stick
- You're a freelancer, coach, or creator with a simple portfolio
- You have under 5 pages of content and no complex functionality

Tools like Framer, Webflow, and Squarespace produce beautiful results quickly. They handle hosting, SSL, and basic SEO out of the box.

**The ceiling hits when you need:**
- Custom booking / enquiry logic
- Product catalogues with 50+ items
- Integration with Indian payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU)
- GST invoicing tied to website purchases
- WhatsApp chat integration with lead routing
- Multi-language support (Tamil/Hindi + English)
- Page speed scores that actually rank on Google India

## The SEO Problem With AI Builders

Google's Core Web Vitals are now a ranking factor. AI-built websites, particularly those generated on Wix and Squarespace, routinely score 40–60 on PageSpeed Insights. A well-built Next.js or React site scores 90+.

In competitive search terms — "web design company Coimbatore", "GST billing software India" — a 40-point PageSpeed difference is often the difference between page 1 and page 3.

## What Custom Development Actually Delivers

A custom-built website (Next.js, React, or even a well-configured WordPress) gives you:

- **Full control** over every pixel, interaction, and user flow
- **Performance** that AI builders can't match
- **Integrations** with any API, payment gateway, or internal system
- **Scalability** — add features as your business grows
- **Ownership** — you're not locked into a platform's pricing forever

## The Decision Framework

Ask yourself one question: **Is my website a cost centre or a revenue channel?**

If it's just a digital business card — builder is fine.

If it's supposed to generate enquiries, rank on Google, and convert visitors into customers — you need custom.
