## AI Is No Longer Just for Big Companies

Two years ago, AI tools were expensive, complex, and designed for enterprises with dedicated tech teams. In 2025, a chai shop owner in Coimbatore can use the same AI tools as a Fortune 500 company — for free or under ₹2,000/month.

This is a fundamental shift. And if your competitors are using it and you're not, you're already behind.

## The AI Tools Actually Worth Using

### 1. ChatGPT / Claude — Your 24/7 Business Assistant

Use it for:
- Drafting emails, proposals, and follow-ups in minutes
- Writing product descriptions for your website or catalogue
- Summarising long contracts, reports, or meeting notes
- Answering customer queries via a trained chatbot on your website

**Cost:** Free tier available. ChatGPT Plus ≈ ₹1,650/month.

### 2. Google Gemini — Integrated with Your Existing Tools

If your business already uses Gmail, Google Docs, or Google Sheets, Gemini is built right in. Ask it to summarise your inbox, draft a reply, or analyse a spreadsheet — without switching apps.

**Best for:** Businesses already in the Google ecosystem.

### 3. Canva AI — Design Without a Designer

Canva's AI features (Magic Design, Background Remover, Text to Image) let you create professional social media posts, brochures, and banners in under 10 minutes.

**Cost:** Free tier is powerful. Canva Pro ≈ ₹4,000/year.

### 4. Notion AI — Turn Chaos Into Organised Systems

Use Notion AI to build internal wikis, SOPs, meeting templates, and project trackers that your whole team can use. The AI summarises, drafts, and organises for you.

### 5. Zoho Zia — AI Built for Indian Businesses

Zoho's AI assistant, Zia, is embedded across their CRM, Books, and Analytics products. It predicts sales, flags anomalies in your accounts, and suggests the best time to contact leads.

**Why it matters for India:** Zoho is GST-compliant, built for Indian tax structures, and priced in rupees.

## What AI Cannot Replace (Yet)

AI is a tool, not a strategy. It cannot:
- Build genuine customer relationships
- Make judgment calls on complex business decisions
- Replace the trust that comes from years of local reputation
- Understand the nuances of your specific market

Use AI to handle the repetitive, time-consuming work — so you can focus on the things only you can do.

## Getting Started This Week

1. Sign up for a free ChatGPT account
2. Ask it to rewrite your "About Us" page
3. Use Canva AI to create 3 social media posts
4. Measure the time you saved

That's it. No complex setup. No IT team needed. Just start small and build from there.
