## First Impressions Happen Online

In 2025, when a potential customer hears about your business, the first thing they do is Google you. Within 50 milliseconds of landing on your site, they've already formed an opinion. No website — or a broken, outdated one — tells them you're not serious.

## What a Professional Website Actually Does

A professional website isn't a brochure. It's your best salesperson — working 24/7, answering questions, building trust, and converting visitors into enquiries.

Here's what it does for your business:

- **Builds credibility** — 75% of users judge a company's credibility based on its website design
- **Captures leads** — contact forms, WhatsApp buttons, and CTAs that convert
- **Ranks on Google** — proper SEO means customers find you before they find competitors
- **Works on mobile** — 60%+ of searches happen on phones

## The Real Cost of Not Having One

A bad website costs you more than you think:

- Lost enquiries going to competitors
- Looking unprofessional to potential partners or investors
- Missing out on Google's local search results

If your competitor has a clean, fast website and you don't — they win. Every time.

## What to Look for in a Web Partner

Not all web agencies are equal. Look for:

1. **Direct developer access** — not a middleman account manager
2. **Mobile-first design** — not a desktop site squeezed onto a phone
3. **Performance** — Lighthouse scores above 90
4. **Post-launch support** — someone to call when something breaks
