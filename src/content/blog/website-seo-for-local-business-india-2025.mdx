## The Single Biggest Opportunity for Indian Local Businesses

"[Service] near me" searches have grown over 400% in the last three years in India. When someone in Coimbatore searches "web design company near me" or "CA near me" or "interior designer Coimbatore", Google shows a map pack of 3 local businesses before any website results.

Getting into that map pack — and keeping your position — is the highest ROI marketing activity available to most Indian local businesses. It's free. It drives calls, directions, and website visits 24/7.

## Step 1: Claim and Fully Optimise Your Google Business Profile

This is non-negotiable. If you haven't claimed your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business), do it today before reading further.

**What fully optimised looks like:**
- Business name exactly as it appears on your signboard and GST certificate
- Primary and secondary categories set correctly (very important)
- Address with pin code — must match your website exactly
- All phone numbers listed, including WhatsApp
- Business hours including holidays
- 20+ high-quality photos (interior, exterior, team, products/work)
- Services listed with descriptions and prices where possible
- "From the business" description using your top 3–5 keywords naturally

**The reviews factor:** Businesses with 50+ reviews at 4.5+ stars dominate the local pack. Ask every satisfied customer to leave a Google review. Make it easy — send them a direct link via WhatsApp.

## Step 2: Consistent NAP Across the Web

NAP = Name, Address, Phone. Google cross-references your business information across the web. Inconsistencies — slightly different addresses, old phone numbers, name variations — confuse Google's local ranking algorithm.

Audit and fix:
- JustDial listing
- Sulekha listing
- IndiaMART (if applicable)
- Facebook Business page
- Instagram business profile
- Your website's contact page and footer

They must all match exactly.

## Step 3: Local Landing Pages on Your Website

If you serve multiple areas, create a dedicated page for each. A web design company in Coimbatore should have pages optimised for:
- `/web-design-coimbatore`
- `/web-design-tirupur`
- `/web-design-erode`

Each page needs genuine, unique content about that location — not just "find the best web design in [city]" repeated 10 times. Google penalises thin, spammy local pages.

## Step 4: Understand AI Overviews (The New SEO Battlefield)

Google's AI Overview (formerly SGE) now appears at the top of many searches and directly answers questions without users clicking through to websites. This is eating into traffic for informational searches.

**How to appear in AI Overviews:**
- Write comprehensive, factual content that directly answers questions
- Use clear headings, bullet points, and tables
- Include specific data and examples (AI models cite sources with concrete information)
- Build domain authority through backlinks from credible Indian websites

## Step 5: Page Speed on Indian Mobile Networks

Most Indian users are on 4G connections, not fibre. A website that loads in 2 seconds on your office WiFi may take 6–8 seconds on an average mobile connection in a tier-2 city.

Use Google PageSpeed Insights to check your score. Anything below 70 on mobile is hurting your rankings.

## The Local SEO Checklist

**Weekly:**
- [ ] Respond to all new Google reviews (positive and negative)
- [ ] Post one update to Google Business Profile (event, offer, or photo)

**Monthly:**
- [ ] Add 5 new photos to Google Business Profile
- [ ] Check for and respond to new questions in Q&A section
- [ ] Review which search terms are bringing visitors to your site (Google Search Console)

**Quarterly:**
- [ ] Audit your NAP consistency across all directories
- [ ] Create or update one local landing page
- [ ] Review and update your top 5 service pages

## Expected Timeline

Local SEO is not immediate. Realistic expectations:

- **Month 1–2:** Profile fully optimised, basic foundations in place
- **Month 3–4:** Appearing in local pack for less competitive terms
- **Month 6+:** Consistent ranking for target keywords
- **Month 12+:** Dominating local search in your category

The businesses that win at local SEO are the ones that treat it as an ongoing commitment, not a one-time setup.
